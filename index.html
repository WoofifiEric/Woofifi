Why We Started

One treat bag, One flip
One inspiration

One day, we flipped a treat bag over and actually read the label. A list of ingredients none of us could say out loud and it hit us! How were we feeding this to the ones we love most?

They ask for nothing but give everything. Every bad day you come home to, they are already at the door. They are not just pets. They are family!

That is where Woofifi was born, real ingredients, whole and honest, the kind where you flip the bag over and actually know exactly what is inside. Nothing more complicated than that.
