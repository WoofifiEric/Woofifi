Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

Woofifi Pte Ltd ("Woofifi", "we", "us" or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit www.woofifi.com, use our mobile application, or purchase our products. This policy is designed to comply with Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Contact information — name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Pet information — pet name, breed, age (optional, used to personalise recommendations)
  • Order & payment information — transaction history, payment method (processed by secure third-party providers; we do not store full card details)
  • Communications — messages sent via contact form, email, WhatsApp or social media
  • Usage data — browser type, device information, pages visited, collected via cookies and analytics tools

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Fulfil orders, arrange delivery, and provide customer support
  • Send newsletters, product updates and promotional offers (you may unsubscribe anytime)
  • Personalise product recommendations for your pet
  • Improve our website, app and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — payment processors, logistics partners, email marketing tools, analytics platforms
  • Certified groomers (Phase 2 app) — only the information needed to fulfil your booking
  • Legal authorities — when required by Singapore law or court order

4. Newsletter & Marketing Communications

When you subscribe to our newsletter, request early access, or sign up for product drops via any form on our website, you are providing consent under the PDPA for Woofifi to send you marketing communications. This includes:

  • What we collect — the email address you submit, the date and source of signup (e.g. homepage form, blog footer), and basic engagement data such as opens and clicks if our email tool reports it
  • What we send — product launches, seasonal and festival drops, restock alerts, occasional promotions, and stories from our kitchen. We do not send daily mail and we do not share your email with other brands
  • Email service providers — subscriber lists are stored and delivered through reputable third-party email platforms (such as Mailchimp, ConvertKit or similar) which act as our data processors and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations
  • How to withdraw consent — every email includes an unsubscribe link, or you can email contact@woofifi.com at any time to be removed. Unsubscribing from marketing does not affect transactional messages tied to an active order
  • Retention — we keep your subscription record for as long as you remain subscribed plus a short period after unsubscribe to suppress future sends, after which the record is purged in line with section 6 below
  • Eligibility — by subscribing you confirm you are at least 18 years old (or the age of majority where you live) and that the email address belongs to you

Subscribing to our newsletter is voluntary and is not a condition of purchasing any product. You may unsubscribe at any time without affecting your customer account or order history.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to remember preferences, analyse traffic, and personalise content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including SSL encryption and secure cloud storage. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by Singapore law. Order records are retained for at least five (5) years in line with IRAS requirements.

8. Your Rights Under PDPA

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Withdraw consent to specific uses (e.g. marketing)
  • Request deletion, subject to legal retention obligations

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@woofifi.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

Woofifi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website.
