Our Story

It started with curiosity.
It ended with Woofifi.

That was the day Woofifi was born.

How it started

It started with a label that broke our hearts a little

You know that moment. You flip a treat bag over and squint at the back. A list of ingredients none of us could read out loud without stumbling over half of them.

That hit harder than we expected. Every morning without fail they are beside us before our alarms go off. They give us their whole day, their whole heart, every single time. And in return, we were handing them a bag of question marks.

So why were we feeding it to the ones who love us most? Not pets. Babies. Family. The ones who steal the couch, photobomb every picture, and somehow always know when you’re having a bad day.

So we got a little obsessed. We wanted treats made from real, whole, natural ingredients. Nothing hidden. Nothing synthetic. The kind of thing you could read out loud without stumbling over a single word. No fancy machines. No shortcuts. No mystery processes. Just nutrient-packed snacks. Slow. Gentle. Done right. Every bite keeps all the good stuff your pet actually needs.

Simple idea. So we made it ourselves, in small batches, in our own kitchen, tested by the four-legged critics who started this whole thing.

Then another thought hit us. We live in a multi-cultural city, the kind that finds an excuse to celebrate every other month. Reunion dinners that stretch past midnight when the lanterns go up for Lunar New Year. Mooncakes shared under a fat Mid-Autumn moon. Diyas, rangoli, and warm sweets through Deepavali. Christmas mornings buried in wrapping paper. Open houses and ketupat that travel the whole island for Hari Raya. But every single time, our fur babies sat on the sidelines. Watching us eat mooncakes. Watching us pass around the goodies. Tails wagging, eyes doing that hopeful thing they do, just wanting to be part of it.

So why shouldn’t they celebrate too? That is exactly why Woofifi has a festival collection. Limited-edition treats made for the moments that matter most, so our fur babies get to join the celebration with us. Tail-wagging mooncakes when the lanterns come out. A bag for every season. A festival every fur baby can actually join. Because the way they see the world is simple. If we are happy, they are happy. If we are home, they are home. Every time we sit down to celebrate, they have already pulled up a spot at our feet, ready for their share. So we make sure they get one. Their own bowl. Their own bag. Their own little version of the joy we are passing around.

Woofifi is not really about treats. It is about treating family the way family deserves. That is why every bag is made from real, whole, natural ingredients. And it is why we built our festival collection. So the ones who love us most never have to sit on the sidelines while the rest of us celebrate.

Our pets gave us the purpose
You give us the reason to keep going

None of us are here for the industry. We are here for the intention behind it.
